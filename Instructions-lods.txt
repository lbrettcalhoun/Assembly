The lods instruction:  Loads a string into the EAX register
	- lodsb:  Load a byte into the AL register
	- lodsw:  Load a word (2 bytes) into the AX register
	- lodsl:  Load a double word (4 bytes) into the EAX register

ESI:  Contains address of source string
DF:   Direction flag controls whether ESI is automatically incremented or decremented
